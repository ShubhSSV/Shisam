Plant Information


Plant Name: Aloe Vera


Benefits: Air purification: Money plants are known to remove indoor pollutants like formaldehyde, benzene, and xylene, improving air quality. Low maintenance: They require minimal care, making them ideal for indoor and outdoor spaces. Positive energy: According to Feng Shui and Vastu Shastra, money plants are believed to bring prosperity, good luck, and positive energy into a home. Stress reduction: Being around greenery and nature can help reduce stress and promote relaxation.



Season: Money plants can thrive year-round, both indoors and outdoors, as they are tropical plants. They grow continuously without a strict seasonal requirement, provided they receive sufficient light and water.



Region: Money plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. However, they can grow almost anywhere in the world as houseplants or in gardens, provided the environment is warm and humid enough.



Age: Lifespan: Money plants can live for 10-15 years or more, especially in controlled indoor environments. They grow rapidly, especially when provided with proper care, light, and watering.
